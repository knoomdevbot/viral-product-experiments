# Launch plan: Friend-profile ShowDrop seed test

Date: 2026-06-29 PDT

## Launch mode

Manual seed test only. Do not run paid ads yet.

## Target sample

- 10–20 individual testers, or
- 3–5 intact friend groups.

Prefer people who already share memes, personality tests, TikTok links, or group-chat games.

## Distribution channels

1. Direct DMs to trusted testers.
2. Small group chats where consent/comfort is clear.
3. Optional: one creator/fandom Discord if we have permission to ask for prototype feedback.

## Test script

> I’m testing a tiny web-game idea: make a fake show starring your friend group. Use nicknames/personas, not anything private. Can you make one with 3–5 people and tell me: would you send the result to the group chat?

Follow-up questions:

1. What did you think this was before using it?
2. Did adding people feel fun or annoying?
3. Did the persona/avatar field make it feel more personal?
4. Would you share the result? Why or why not?
5. What template would you want next?

## Copy angles to test

### Angle A — friend group game

Turn your group chat into a fake show. Add 3 friends, get roles, drama, and an Episode 2 unlock.

### Angle B — you are in this

I made a mini-show and you’re in it. Claim your role before Episode 2 drops.

### Angle C — persona-safe version

Use fictional personas or emoji avatars to make a chaotic show poster for your group.

## Do not do yet

- No public ad spend.
- No social scraping.
- No contact import.
- No automatic image grabbing from Instagram/TikTok/Google.
- No claim that real people endorsed their generated roles.

## Next-slice launch if test works

- Add hosted analytics.
- Add share-card PNG export.
- Add remix CTA on recipient view.
- Add template picker landing page.
- Add explicit consent copy around profile images.
